NewsHound CustomGPT Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when using our Custom GPT. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring your data is secure and used appropriately.

Data Collection

We do not store or save any data you provide while using the Custom GPT. All interactions are processed in real-time to generate responses and are not retained or accessible after your session ends.

Data Usage

Your inputs are used solely to generate responses during your session. These inputs are not used for training OpenAI's models, as we have disabled data collection for model improvement purposes.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Your interactions remain private and are not accessible to anyone other than yourself during the session.

Admin Access

As the creator of this Custom GPT, we do not have access to your chats or any data entered during your session unless you explicitly choose to share it with us.
